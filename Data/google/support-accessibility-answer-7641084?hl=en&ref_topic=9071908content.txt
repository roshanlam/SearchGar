Be My Eyes app instructions

On your Android phone or tablet, open the Google Play Store app .
In the search bar, search for the Be My Eyes app.
Tap Install.
Tap Open.
Review the Be My Eyes Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, then tap I Agree.
Complete the individual user setup.
Tap Specialized Help  Google.
Tap Call Google.
The first time you call, review the Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, then tap I Agree.
A call begins automatically, connecting you with a Google Disability Support specialist.
