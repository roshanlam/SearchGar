Google Chat


                  Simplify 1:1 messaging and group collaboration
                

From direct messages to group conversations, Google
                  Chat and Spaces help teams collaborate fluidly and efficiently from anywhere.
                  Securely connect with anyone you work with, and take group work to the next level
                  with shared chat, files and tasks.
                  




                    Get started


Help
















                  See all of your messages in one place
                


                    Use Chat right in Gmail, or as a standalone app. Seamlessly integrated tools
                    help you keep track of all your work communications and choose the right
                    channel for every conversation.
                  

















                  Collaborate in dedicated Spaces
                


                    Spaces are the central place for teams to engage in topic-based discussions,
                    share knowledge and ideas, move projects forward and build communities and team
                    culture.
                  

















                  Tap into the best of Google Workspace
                


                    Use Chat and Spaces to collaborate seamlessly on content with Google Docs,
                    Sheets, and Slides. Easily find past conversations or files with Googleâs
                    powerful search, and let bots and smart suggestions help you connect with
                    meeting participants and get more done, faster.
                  

















                  Search with the power of Google
                

                  Powerful, filterable search across Chat and email threads makes it easy to find
                  what you need, when you need it. Search acts as a collective team memory,
                  allowing you to review past discussions and decisions.
                





                  Protect your data
                

                  Take advantage of the same secure-by-design infrastructure, built-in protection,
                  and global network that Google uses to secure your
                  information and safeguard your privacy. We
                  encrypt your data in transit & at-rest, and our array of default-on anti-abuse
                  measures helps keep your organization safe.
                





                  Smarter chat with bots
                

                  Easily integrate both Google and third-party apps - like Salesforce and Jira - to
                  keep all of your notifications in one place, and use bots to automate simple
                  tasks so you can focus on what matters. Get started with bots.
